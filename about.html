About us

We do not function as distributors or operate as a traditional shop. Our purpose is to facilitate the import of graphics cards from
China at the most favorable rates for you. Acting as an importer, our aim is to maintain a minimal profit margin while ensuring
your product's safe delivery. However, it is important to understand that we are unable to offer product returns or warranty claims.
Our commitment lies in safeguarding your product during shipping and providing you with the best possible price. While we can
assure the physical safety of the shipment, please be aware that handling technical issues, such as warranty claims or returns,
is beyond our capabilities.
